St. Clare's Book 1

The Twins at St Clare's is a children's novel by Enid Blyton set in an English girls' boarding school.Pat and Isabel find life at St Clare's is not as easy as they thought. They have several surprises and arguments before they admit their mistakes, and settle down to make friends.

St Clare's Book 2

In book two, it's the start of the Easter Term and the twins are looking forward to meeting all their friends at St Clare's once more. They are determined to be obedient and studious, but the new girls prove to be so much fun.Expect mischief at St Clare's!

St Clare's Book 3

In book three, Pat and Isabel look forward to their first summer term at St Clare's. They soon make friends with new girl Carlotta, who has a secret past that she's desperate to hide. But sneaky new girl Prudence finds out and tells everybody. How will the class react? There'll be mischief at St Clare's!
